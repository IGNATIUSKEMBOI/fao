Welcome to Sergoit

A Community Built on Knowledge & Character

Nestled in the lush highlands of Eldoret, Sergoit Primary School has been a beacon of quality education since 1999. We offer a nurturing environment where every child is seen, supported, and inspired to achieve their very best.

Under the CBC curriculum framework, we blend academic rigour with co-curricular excellence — from music and cultural arts to sports and community service.

📖

Academic Excellence

Consistently top-performing in KCPE and county assessments, with personalised learning support for every student.

🌿

Holistic Development

Sports, arts, leadership, and community values are woven into daily school life alongside academics.

🤝

Inclusive Community

A safe, welcoming environment that celebrates every child's culture, talent, and unique potential.

What We Offer

Academic Programs & Activities

Comprehensive learning experiences that prepare students for secondary school and beyond.

📚

Lower Primary (PP1 – Grade 3)

Play-based learning that builds strong literacy, numeracy, and social foundations under CBC guidelines. Every child starts with a love of learning.

🔬

Upper Primary (Grade 4 – 9)

Rigorous academic preparation across all subjects — Science, Mathematics, English, Kiswahili, Social Studies, and Creative Arts — with KCPE excellence.

⚽

Sports & Athletics

Football, athletics, netball, and volleyball clubs led by qualified coaches. We regularly compete at county and national levels with pride.

🎵

Music & Cultural Arts

Award-winning choir, traditional dance, drama, and visual arts programs celebrating Kenya's rich cultural heritage and building creative confidence.

💻

Digital Literacy

Computer lab equipped for foundational ICT skills, preparing students for a technology-driven future and secondary school requirements.

🤝

Life Skills & Leadership

Scouting, student council, environmental clubs, and community service programmes build responsible, ethical, and empathetic young leaders.
